Our Story

We started by selling shoes as if we were only going to offer the shoes one order at a time until we were recognized and recognized that we were okay selling shoes and according to that we made a lot of money and we were able to build our own place and shop. thus this Tetra Sole was formed

The Tetra Sole is a new small shoe business and was derived from the word tetrad which means a group of four and Sole comes from the shoe part itself. The Tetra Sole was founded by a group of friends who all share the same passion for shoes and fashion, who want to make beautiful, comfortable and stylish footwear that fits your feet perfectly. It's a brand that sticks to its simple design but with a twist of creativity.

Shoe Experts

Leonardo da Vinci described the human foot as "a masterpiece of engineering and a work of art". And to make sure we can look after it better than anyone else, we've studied it. From the cradle to old age. We've looked at the way it develops. We've scrutinised its biomechanics, the way it moves at work, rest and play. We've deliberated on what it takes to keep feet healthy and happy. And what we've learned is what helps us make the perfect shoes for you.
